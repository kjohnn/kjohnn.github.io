About W Generator

Summary

W Generator is an easy-to-use app that can play various types of waves including sine wave from 20Hz to 20kHz. Moreover it provides waves with note names and is able to play many chords with different kinds of waves. The main screen is shown in the figure1. Lower frequency wave from 20Hz to 100Hz would be too quiet to be heard due to frequency characteristics of iPhone’s or iPad’s default speakers, which would cause higher frequency wave from 18kHz to 20kHz to be so. Higher frequency wave might not be heard because of aged high frequency loss. However, with headphones or add-on speakers which have good frequency characteristics, lower and higher frequency waves might be heard more clearly.

Caution

Extremely High volume could damage your ears using headphones or add-on speakers.

Features

Wave forms:
Sine, Triangle, Sawtooth, Reverse-Sawtooth, Square and Half-wave Rectification
Modes:
Frequency, Note and Chord
Notes:
C0 to B9
Chord types:
6, 7, 69, M7, add9, M9, aug, dim, aug7 and sus4
Gain:
0.0dB to -99.0dB
