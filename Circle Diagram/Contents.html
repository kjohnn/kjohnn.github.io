About Circle Diagram

Summary

Circle Diagram is an easy-to-use app that can show the circle diagram of instrument voltage transformers given their zero burden error and rated burden error. And it enables you to get their error with an arbitrary burden you would connect to their secondary terminals. The error means ratio error and phase angle error of instrument voltage transformers. The zero burden error is one of their errors with the secondary terminals open. The rated burden error is one of their errors with the secondary terminals connecting to the rated burden. You can move a pointer within the error circle on the diagram and get the burden and the power factor with the arbitrary burden error. The main screen is shown in the figure 1. It has a circle diagram with ratio error on the x-axis and phase angle error on the y-axis. And it has the zero burden and its error TextFields, the rated burden and its error TextFields on the bottom and the arbitrary burden and its error TextFields on the top. The zero burden and the rated burden and their error are used to calculate the circle. You can get the error with the arbitrary burden and the location of the pointer which means the ratio error and the phase angle error on the diagram. The line, cusor and letter color on the diagram could be changed into the color you would like in the settings Action Sheet which could be shown by tapping the Settings Icon. The line width on the diagram could be changed also in the sheet.

Specifications

Ratio Error:
-2.00 % to +2.00 %
Phase Angle Error:
-68.80 minutes to +68.80 minutes
Arbitrary Burden:
0.01 VA to Rated Burden
Rated Burden:
0.01 VA to 999.99 VA
Power Factor:
0.00 to 1.00
