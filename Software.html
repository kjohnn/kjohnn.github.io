W Generator 1.1.0

W Generator is an easy-to-use app that can play various types of waves including sine wave from 20Hz to 20kHz. Moreover it provides waves with note names and is able to play many chords with different kinds of waves.
Circle Diagram 1.2.0

Circle Diagram is an easy-to-use app that can show the circle diagram of instrument voltage transformers given their zero burden errors and rated burden errors. And it enables you to get their errors with an arbitrary burden you would connect to their secondary terminals.
